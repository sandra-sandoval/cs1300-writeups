Ethical Design of Period Tracking Apps

Overview
When looking at current Period Tracking applications, it is clear that many have similar features aiming at giving their users a better understanding of their menstrual health. Over the past few years, however, users have become more concern with how their usage of these apps could be used against them. This pushed me to consider the social impacts of common features and design a period tracking app that accounts for them. I will develop a lo-fi mockup of a period tracking app that is user conscious and considers the ethical implications of its features.

Social Impacts
While there are several social implications for common period app features, I want to specifically discuss and prioritize these issues in my design.
